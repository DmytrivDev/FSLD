    04.04.2024 09:00 - 13:00

    Internally displaced persons. Solidarity without borders

    The war in Ukraine has caused the largest mass displacement of people in Europe since World War II. We will discuss how to overcome its consequences together; projects that will help IDPs adapt and at the same time preserve their Ukrainian identity. And most importantly, how we can all come out of this crisis stronger.

    Restoration of health - physical and mental

    Constant stress caused by the war, loss of loved ones, home, stability - all this destroys the physical and mental health of the nation. We will discuss the restoration of a healthy society through the rebuilding of the Ukrainian healthcare system, mental health and rehabilitation programs, medical evacuation, and the best international health support programs.

  • Panel 5
    04.04.2024 09:00 - 13:00

    The female factor of recovery

    Ukrainian women are also on the front lines - and not just in the Armed Forces. They are doctors, teachers, volunteers, and mothers who are making extraordinary efforts to keep up on all fronts in the midst of war. This session will focus on the special mission of women in building the modern world, women's leadership during the war, and their role in post-war recovery.
